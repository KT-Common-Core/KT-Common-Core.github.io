Why do families choose Khan's?

No matter what grade your child is in, Khan's will establish all
fundamental Common Core standards and provide a unique education for each student.

3rd Grade

For students taking standardized tests for the first time, the experience can be uncomfortable. We provide our 3rd grade students with extra practice through our signature diagnostic exams to ensure that they'll be comfortable when testing day comes around.

4th Grade

The Common Core exam determines which Middle school your child is accepted into so we will ensure your child completely understands all the standards. In addition, the NYS Science exam is administered in 4th grade so we will cover the science curriculum as well.

5th Grade

The 5th Grade Common Core program covers all aspects of the Common Core curriculum and prepares students for Middle school Admissions. Perfect scorers of 4/4 qualify for the Hunter College High School exam which can set your child up for college.

6th Grade

Stepping into Middle school can be an uncomfortable experience. The KT Common Core program for 6th grade will assist with the transition from Elementary school to Middle school. Additionally, at the request of families we will prepare for the Hunter exam.

7th Grade

The 7th grade exam is essential to the High School admissions process and we will ensure your child is prepared for these exams. In addition, our program provides Pre-SHSAT education, giving students a head start with a strong foundation.
